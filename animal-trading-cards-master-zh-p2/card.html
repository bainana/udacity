Parrot

Characteristic features of parrots include a strong, curved bill, an upright stance, strong legs, and clawed zygodactyl feet.

  • scientific: Psittacines
  • average Length: 100cm
  • average lifespan: 50-60years
  • habitat: Tropical area

Parrots, also known as psittacines /ˈsɪtəsaɪnz/,[2][3] are birds of the roughly 393 species in 92 genera that make up the order Psittaciformes, found in most tropical and subtropical regions.
